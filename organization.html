WBNHS Organizations

Discover the various organizations that contribute to the vibrant student life at Western Bicutan National High School. Learn more about their goals and contributions.

Commission on Election and Appointments

This organization ensures the integrity and transparency of student elections and appointments. It fosters student leadership through democratic practices.

Campus Integrity Crusaders

Promotes ethical leadership, moral values, and honesty, ensuring students embody trustworthiness in all school activities.

M.A.L.A.Y.A.

Meaningful Actions Leads to Anti-Discrimination Youth Association advocates for equality and inclusivity, empowering students to fight discrimination and celebrate diversity.

Marian Youth Movement

A faith-based organization dedicated to nurturing spirituality among students, with a focus on devotion to Mary and Christian values.

Batang Emergency Response Team

Focused on disaster preparedness, this team trains students to respond effectively to emergencies, promoting safety and readiness in the school and community.

Society of English Enthusiasts

This group enhances students' English proficiency through public speaking, writing, and literature appreciation activities, fostering communication skills and creativity.

Citizenship Advancement Training

Developing leadership and civic responsibility, this program encourages students to engage in community service and national development initiatives.

Math Enthusiasts

A club for students passionate about mathematics, promoting problem-solving and critical thinking through fun and challenging activities.

Campus Wellness Program

Dedicated to promoting health and wellness among students, this program focuses on physical fitness, nutrition, and mental well-being.

Siyen-Saya

Science for Aspiring Young Achievers (Siyen-Saya) nurtures scientific curiosity and innovation through experiments, research, and interactive learning.

Scouting

Instilling discipline, survival skills, and a sense of camaraderie, this organization promotes outdoor adventures and leadership training among students.
